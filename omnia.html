Frequently asked questions

What is Omnia?

Omnia is an AI visibility platform that shows where and how your brand appears in AI search engines like ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google AI Overviews, and Google AI mode.

Unlike most AI visibility tools, Omnia also takes a step further and shows you how to act on your data to get featured in future AI answer.

What AI engines can I track with Omnia?

Omnia tracks the major AI engines out there, like ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google AI Overviews, and Google AI mode.

We choose to deliberately not track every AI engine out there, and consider these ones the most relevant at this moment in time, with others just adding to the noise.

How often do you track prompts and refresh data?

Omnia tracks prompts every 24 hours so you can spot changes in AI as they happen. We have clients that see results in as little as 7 days, and use daily tracking to give you quick feedback loops to act on.

How do I know if AI recommends my product?

Omnia runs prompts to find out and show you whether your brand is mentioned in AI answers, including how you benchmark against competitors.

How does Omnia track brand mentions in AI answers?

Omnia mimics real user behaviour to runs prompts that users type to learn about your category and product.

What are the best AI search monitoring tools?

Most tools only test APIs or sample answers. Omnia focuses on real AI search experiences, real prompts that users type to find products like yours.

What is Answer Engine Optimization (AEO)?

AEO is the practice of optimizing your brand’s online presence to be mentioned and recommended in AI-generated answers instead of the traditional ranking blue links.

How is AEO different from SEO?

SEO optimizes for clicks from search results. AEO optimizes for mentions and recommendations inside AI answers, often without a click.

How long does it take to see results from AEO?

We’ve seen results in AEO in as little as 7 days with customers. Unlike SEO, which often takes months, AI visibility can be changed and influenced much more quickly.

How do AI search engines decide which brands to recommend?

They rely on authority signals like brand mentions, citations, content clarity, structured data, and third-party validation, not just keywords or backlinks.

Omnia helps brands discover high‑demand topics in AI assistants, monitor their positioning, understand the sources those assistants cite, and launch agents to create and place AI‑optimized content where it matters.
